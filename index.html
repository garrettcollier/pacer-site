About Pacer LTD

About

Trusted jewelry lending, handled professionally.

Pacer LTD specializes exclusively in short-term loans secured by fine jewelry. If you need access to capital and prefer not to work with a bank, you can work directly with Tim Collier.

Each item is professionally evaluated and stored in a private safety deposit vault for the duration of the loan. Once the advance is repaid, the jewelry is returned in full. The process is discreet, appointment-based, and designed to protect both privacy and value.
